积雪质量平衡

各相质量平衡

$a**2+y=12$

θi + θv + θl = 1

在一维尺度上,
$$\frac{\partial\rho_{t}}{\partial t}=\frac{\partial\rho_{v}}{\partial t}-\frac{\partial\rho_{l}}{\partial t}$$

针对雪层中的每一相，质量平衡方程可以写为，

对于冰：
$$\frac{\partial\rho_{i}\theta_{i}}{\partial t}+\dot{M_{iv}}+\dot{M_{il}}=0$$

对于液态水：
$$\frac{\partial\rho_{l}\theta_{l}}{\partial t}
+\frac{\partial U_{l}}{\partial z}+\dot{M_{lv}}-\dot{M_{il}}=0$$

这里Ul 是水流通量 (k**g ⋅ m−2 ⋅ s).
θi是体积含冰量
(m3 ⋅ m−3).

对于水汽，
$$\frac{\partial\rho_{v}\theta_{v}}{\partial t}
+\dot{M_{lv}}-\dot{M_{iv}}=\frac{\partial (D_{eff}\frac{\partial\rho_{v}}{\partial z })}{\partial z}$$

水汽温度可近似为雪层温度.

雪层中水流过程

In the momentum equation, the inertial, convection and phase change and
pressure are small,so the equation become:
$$\rho_{l}\theta_{l}g=\frac{\theta_{l}^2\mu_{l}v_l}{K_l}$$
Where
μl = 1.787 × 10( − 3)N ⋅ s/m2
is the dynamic viscosity of water at 0∘C.

we can get the velocity vector :
$$v_l=-\frac{K_{l}\theta_{l}g}{\theta_{l}\mu_l}$$
then the mass water flux:
$$U_l=\rho_l\theta_{l}v_l=-\frac{K_l}{\mu_l}\rho_{l}^{2}g$$
where the Kl is hydraulic permeability,which can be
expressed in the terms of the effective liquid saturations.

effective liquid saturation se:

$$s_e=\frac{s-s_r}{1-s_r}$$

where sr is the irreducible water saturation,research
show the value of the irreducible water content
θ = srϕ is from 0.0-0.4, Colbeck(1974b) suggested a
value of sr=0.07, and sr=0.04 in the
Jordan(1991).

From the Jodran(1991),the Kl can be expressed as,
Kl = Kmaxse3
Kmax = 0.077d2e−0.0078ρiθi
